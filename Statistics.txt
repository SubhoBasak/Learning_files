Statistics is the stude of how to collect, organize, analyze and visualize numerical information and data.

Individuals are people or objects included in study. 5 individuals could be 5 people, or 5 records, or 5 reports.

Variable is the characteristic of individual to be measured or observed.

Population is the entire group of individuals with a common theme.

Sample is some part of population, representing the entire population.

Some notation in statistics :
	N - entire population
	n - sample of the population

Parameter is a measure, describes about the population.

Statistics is a measure, describe about the sample.
